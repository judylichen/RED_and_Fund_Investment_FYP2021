11. What do you think about taking social media post as a reference to make investment decision/你如何看待參考社交媒體的信息做投資決策的這種情況?

a. Not reliable at all/社交媒體不可信
b. There are too many similar posts. I cannot identify which is original and hard to tell its reliability./社交媒體上太多相似文章，我無法分辨原創並且很難了解其可信度。
c. I think those KOLs who have many followers/ higher engagement rate are relatively reliable than others./我認為有很多追蹤者或很高互動頻率的‘網紅’相對比較可信。
d. Social media is just a platform for them to share their ideas. We can take as a reference but not imitating them. /社交媒體只是讓人們分享想法的地方，我們可以參考但不能全信。
e. We can follow their steps on make our own investment decisions. /我們可以模仿他們的投資決策。
